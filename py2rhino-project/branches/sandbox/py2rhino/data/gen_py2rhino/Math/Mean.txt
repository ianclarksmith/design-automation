[Mean]

    Returns the mean number, or average, from an array of numbers.

[Syntax]

    Rhino.Mean (arrNumbers)

[Parameters]

    arrNumbers
    Required.  Array.  An array of numbers to analyze.

[Returns]

    Number
    The mean number if successful.
    Null
    If not successful, or on error.

[Example]

    MsgBox Rhino.Mean(Array(1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9))
