[StatusBarPoint]

    Sets Rhino's status bar point coordinate panes.

[Syntax]

    Rhino.StatusBarPoint ([arrPoint])

[Parameters]

    arrPoint
    Optional.  Array.  A 3-D point.

[Returns]

    Null.

[Example]

    Dim pt
    pt = Array(1.1, 2.2, 3.3)
    Rhino.StatusBarPoint pt

[Also See]

    StatusBarDistance
    StatusBarMessage
    StatusBarNumber
